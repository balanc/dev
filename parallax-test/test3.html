Day in the Life of an Idea

The first idea is a brave one. It's sometimes difficult to get it out of your head and in the world where someone could see it. But once it comes out, many more will follow its steps without hesitation.

You'll love some and hate many. Keep writing no matter how weird or ridiculous they sound. The only mistake you can make is to not write things down. You'd be surprised which ideas turn out to be the best...

The winners will be made clear with a fresh set of eyes.

It may have been the first idea you had or the last you squeezed out or the one you were so willing to dismiss you almost didn't write it down. All that matters is you found it. It stood up to all the rest. And most importantly, you believe in it. Now, it's up to you to make it into something great.

An idea is just that - an idea. Without you it wouldn't exist. But ideas need to be put into action. They can change the smallest things or the largest problems, but none of it can happen if you don't scribble down the first one at all.
